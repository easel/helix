# Atlassian Vision Creation

## Source

- URL: https://www.atlassian.com/team-playbook/plays/vision-creation
- Accessed: 2026-05-12

## Summary

Atlassian's Vision Creation play treats vision as a shared picture of the
future a team is building. It distinguishes vision from mission, recommends
grounding the work in customer personas and business outcomes, and emphasizes
early alignment before strategy and execution harden.

## Relevant Findings

- Vision should describe the future state the team wants to create.
- A useful vision is larger than short-term business goals but still grounded in
  customer empathy.
- Early vision work is valuable because misalignment is cheaper to find before
  downstream plans solidify.
