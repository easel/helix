# NN/g UX Research Methods

## Source

- URL: https://www.nngroup.com/articles/which-ux-research-methods/
- Accessed: 2026-05-12

## Summary

Nielsen Norman Group explains that different UX research methods answer
different questions and are useful at different product stages. The guidance
maps methods across behavioral versus attitudinal data, qualitative versus
quantitative data, and product-development phase.

## Relevant Findings

- Research methods should be selected according to the question being asked.
- Discovery work benefits from methods that expose user behavior, context, and
  needs before the solution is fixed.
- Qualitative methods help explain why something happens; quantitative methods
  help measure how much or how often it happens.
- A research plan should match method, participant/source, and output to the
  decision the team needs to make.
