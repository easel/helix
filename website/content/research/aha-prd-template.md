# Aha! Product Requirements Document Template

## Source

- URL: https://www.aha.io/roadmapping/guide/templates/create/prd
- Accessed: 2026-05-12

## Summary

Aha! frames the PRD as a concise place to communicate what is being built, who
it is for, and how it delivers customer value. The template guidance emphasizes
alignment with product goals, cross-functional understanding of scope, and
iterative collaboration rather than exhaustive prescription.

## Relevant Findings

- A PRD should define what will be delivered for a product, release, or feature
  set.
- Useful PRDs include objectives, assumptions, scope, ownership, milestones, and
  dependencies only when those details help collaborators.
- The goal is enough information for teammates to create strong solutions, not
  to prescribe every interaction.
- Concision and accessibility matter because many functions use the PRD.
