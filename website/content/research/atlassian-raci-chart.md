# Atlassian RACI Chart

## Source

- URL: https://www.atlassian.com/work-management/project-management/raci-chart
- Accessed: 2026-05-12

## Summary

Atlassian describes a RACI chart as a responsibility assignment matrix that
clarifies who is responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed for project
tasks or decisions. It emphasizes clearer communication, decision making, and
accountability.

## Relevant Findings

- Responsible parties do the work within agreed parameters.
- Accountable parties own the outcome and should generally be singular for a
  task or decision.
- Consulted parties provide input before work or decisions proceed.
- Informed parties need updates but are not expected to provide input.
- RACI helps expose bottlenecks, unclear ownership, and overloaded decision
  paths.
