# Geoffrey Moore Positioning Template

## Source

- URL: https://the.gt/geoffrey-moore-positioning-statement/
- Accessed: 2026-05-12

## Summary

The source presents Geoffrey Moore's positioning statement template from
_Crossing the Chasm_. The template forces a short statement to name the target
customer, need or opportunity, product name, category, key benefit, primary
competitive alternative, and primary differentiation.

## Relevant Findings

- Positioning is strongest when the target customer is concrete.
- The current alternative must be named; otherwise differentiation is vague.
- Category, benefit, and differentiator should fit in one readable sentence.
