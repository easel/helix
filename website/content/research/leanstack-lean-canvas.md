# LEANSTACK Lean Canvas

## Source

- URL: https://leanstack.com/
- Accessed: 2026-05-12

## Summary

LEANSTACK, founded by Ash Maurya, presents Lean Canvas as a one-page business
model format adapted for startups and early ideas. Its public guidance stresses
loving the problem rather than the solution, treating validation speed as an
advantage, and spending scarce time on validation instead of perfection.

## Relevant Findings

- Early-stage opportunity work should start with the problem, not the proposed
  solution.
- Speed of learning matters because assumptions are still uncertain.
- Traction and validation are more important than polished planning.
- Lean Canvas includes opportunity elements such as problem, solution, key
  metrics, customer segments, and unfair advantage.
