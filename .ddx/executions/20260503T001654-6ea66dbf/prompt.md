Consolidate helix status into a single surface answering: (a) current cycle number, (b) currently-executing bead id and claim age, (c) parked beads classified by reason (dep-blocked, retry-parked with next-eligible-time, orphaned-claim), (d) recently-injected supervisory beads (review-finding, alignment-review) with triggering cycle and triggering bead, (e) stop reason of the last run.
