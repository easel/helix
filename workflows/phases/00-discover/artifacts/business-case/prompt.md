# Business Case Prompt

Create a business case that justifies investment with market sizing, ROI, and risk assessment.

## Storage Location

Store at: `docs/helix/00-discover/business-case.md`

## Purpose

The Business Case answers: **Should we invest in this opportunity?**

It provides:
- Market opportunity quantification
- Investment requirements
- Expected returns
- Risk assessment
- Strategic alignment evidence

## Key Principles

### 1. Quantify Everything
- Use numbers, not adjectives
- Show your assumptions
- Provide ranges for uncertainty

### 2. Be Honest About Risks
- List what could go wrong
- Quantify potential impact
- Show mitigation strategies

### 3. Show Strategic Fit
- Connect to company goals
- Demonstrate resource feasibility
- Explain timing rationale

## Section Guidance

### Opportunity Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- **TAM**: Total addressable market (if you had 100%)
- **SAM**: Serviceable addressable market (your target segment)
- **SOM**: Serviceable obtainable market (realistic capture)
- Show calculation methodology

### Investment Required
- Development costs (team, time)
- Infrastructure and tooling
- Go-to-market costs
- Ongoing operational costs

### Expected ROI
- Revenue projections
- Cost savings (if internal)
- Time to breakeven
- Payback period

### Risk Assessment
- Technical risks
- Market risks
- Competitive risks
- Execution risks
- Mitigation for each

### Strategic Alignment
- How this supports company goals
- Resource availability
- Opportunity cost consideration

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Market sizing uses verifiable data sources
- [ ] Investment estimate is realistic
- [ ] ROI assumptions are documented
- [ ] Major risks identified with mitigations
- [ ] Strategic fit is explicit
