# Product Vision Prompt

Create a concise product vision that aligns stakeholders on mission, direction, and value.

## Storage Location

Store the vision at: `docs/helix/00-discover/product-vision.md`

## Purpose

The Product Vision is a **north star document** that:
- Aligns team on long-term direction
- Communicates value to stakeholders
- Guides prioritization decisions
- Inspires and motivates the team

## Key Principles

### 1. Be Concise
- Mission statement: 1-2 sentences max
- Each section: essential information only
- Remove fluff and corporate speak

### 2. Be Specific
- Name target customers precisely
- Quantify market opportunity
- Define measurable success

### 3. Be Compelling
- Articulate why this matters
- Connect to customer pain
- Show the opportunity

## Section Guidance

### Mission Statement
- What you do + for whom + why it matters
- Under 50 words
- Memorable and quotable

### 3-5 Year Vision
- Where you want to be
- Market position aspired to
- Impact you want to achieve

### Target Market
- Primary customer segment
- Size and characteristics
- Why they need this

### Key Value Propositions
- 3-5 core benefits
- Unique to your offering
- Customer-centric language

### Success Definition
- Primary success metric
- Supporting metrics
- Timeline for achievement

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Mission fits in a tweet
- [ ] Vision is ambitious but achievable
- [ ] Target market is specific (not "everyone")
- [ ] Value props differentiate from alternatives
- [ ] Success metrics are measurable
