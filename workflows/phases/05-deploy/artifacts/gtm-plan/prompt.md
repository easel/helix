# Go-to-Market Plan Generation Prompt

Create a focused GTM plan that coordinates launch activities across marketing, sales, and product teams to maximize market impact.

## Storage Location

Store the GTM plan at: `docs/helix/05-deploy/gtm-plan.md`

## Purpose

The GTM plan serves as:
- A unified launch coordination document
- A messaging and positioning guide
- A channel strategy reference
- A success metrics framework
- A timeline for launch activities

## Key Requirements

### 1. Launch Timeline

#### Pre-Launch (T-30 to T-7)
- Internal enablement complete
- Marketing assets finalized
- Sales materials ready
- Support documentation published
- Beta feedback incorporated
- Press/analyst briefings scheduled

#### Launch Window (T-7 to T+7)
- Soft launch to select customers
- Full public announcement
- Content publishing schedule
- Social media campaign
- Email sequences triggered
- PR activities executed

#### Post-Launch (T+7 to T+30)
- Performance analysis
- Customer feedback collection
- Campaign optimization
- Success story capture
- Iteration planning

### 2. Target Segments

#### Primary Segment
- Segment definition and size
- Key pain points addressed
- Value proposition fit
- Buying behavior
- Decision makers and influencers
- Competitive alternatives

#### Secondary Segments
- Additional market opportunities
- Expansion potential
- Cross-sell possibilities
- Partner channel targets

### 3. Positioning & Messaging

#### Core Positioning
- Category definition
- Key differentiators
- Competitive positioning
- Value proposition statement

#### Messaging Framework
- Headline messaging
- Supporting proof points
- Customer testimonials
- Use case examples
- Objection handling

### 4. Channel Strategy

#### Owned Channels
- Website/landing pages
- Blog/content hub
- Email marketing
- Product in-app
- Documentation

#### Earned Channels
- Press coverage
- Analyst relations
- Customer advocacy
- Social mentions
- Community engagement

#### Paid Channels
- Digital advertising
- Sponsored content
- Event sponsorships
- Paid partnerships

### 5. Success Metrics

#### Awareness Metrics
- Website traffic
- Social reach/engagement
- Press mentions
- Brand search volume

#### Engagement Metrics
- Sign-ups/trials
- Content downloads
- Demo requests
- Event attendance

#### Conversion Metrics
- Trial-to-paid conversion
- Sales pipeline generated
- Revenue from launch
- Customer acquisition cost

## Template Guidelines

### Keep It Actionable
- Specific dates and owners
- Clear deliverables
- Measurable outcomes
- Dependencies mapped

### Align Stakeholders
- Product marketing owns messaging
- Demand gen owns campaigns
- Sales owns enablement
- Product owns release
- Support owns readiness

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing the GTM plan:
- [ ] Timeline includes all dependencies
- [ ] Segments are clearly defined
- [ ] Messaging is customer-validated
- [ ] Channel mix is appropriate
- [ ] Metrics are measurable
- [ ] Resources are assigned
- [ ] Budget is allocated
- [ ] Risks are identified

## Integration with Deploy Phase

This plan coordinates with:
1. **Release Notes** - User-facing feature communication
2. **Launch Checklist** - Cross-functional readiness
3. **Deployment Checklist** - Technical deployment coordination

Remember: A GTM plan is only as good as its execution. Keep it focused, measurable, and owned.
